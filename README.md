# Super Mario World: Challenge Mode

After Mario and Luigi saved the Princess yet again, the 3 of them decide to race and see who can get to the end of Dinosaur Land the fastest!

This ROM hack, inspired by NSMBU’s Challenge Mode, adds character switching, new moves, and some other enhancements to make this better for speedrunning, while retaining some of the original game’s aspects.

This is a ROM hack, meaning you will have to provide and patch your own clean Super Mario World ROM. Super Mario All-Stars+Super Mario World, Yoshi’s Island, or Super Mario Advance 2 will NOT work. The .bps file is provided; combine this with your clean ROM and play on an emulator. This has not been tested on real hardware or clone consoles, like the Analogue Super Nt, so your experience may vary.

Enjoy!
